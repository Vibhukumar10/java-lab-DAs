Mason is working in a big firm called ‘Big Apple’ and you have been assigned a simple task to copy some data from one file to another. But since it is a big IT company and Mason is an intern, he wants to prove himself that he can do this task without opening the files manually, rather, he decides that he will write a Java Program to automate the job for him. Help Mason do his job in order to impress his boss and coworkers at ‘Big Apple’.
